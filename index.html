Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining the session.

Is this webinar free?

Yes, the session is completely free. Our goal is to provide high-quality financial education and build a supportive community of savvy savers across Canada.

Do I need financial experience?

Not at all. This webinar is specifically designed for beginners. We break down complex banking rules, tax accounts, and budgeting tactics into simple, everyday English.

Will there be a replay?

A limited replay will be shared with registered attendees, but we highly recommend attending live to participate in the interactive Q&A sessions.

Can I ask questions?

Absolutely. We have dedicated live Q&A segments where you can ask @yourfinanceguy specific questions about personal budgeting, TFSAs, and emergency savings.

Who should attend?

Anyone looking to build healthier money habits in Canada. This includes newcomers setting up their lives, international students, young professionals, and families wanting clear savings structures.

How long is the webinar?

The core presentation lasts approximately 45 minutes, followed by 15-20 minutes of live Q&A to address your personal scenarios.
